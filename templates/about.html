Our Story

It all began in 1998 when Mr. Kaviarasan started with just 12 cows and a dream to bring pure, honest dairy to the families of Tamil Nadu. Three generations later, Kavi's Dairy has grown into a 500+ cow farm that remains true to its roots — no shortcuts, no compromises.

Every product that leaves our farm is made from milk collected within hours, processed with minimal intervention, and delivered cold to maintain freshness. We believe food should be as close to nature as possible.

Our cows graze freely on pesticide-free green pastures, are never given artificial growth hormones, and are cared for with love every single day.
